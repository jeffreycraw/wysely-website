Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Wysely.

About Wysely

What is Wysely?

Wysely is a sports betting tracker and analytics app that helps you understand your betting performance. It automatically syncs your bet history from FanDuel and DraftKings and shows you key metrics like ROI, win rate, and profit/loss — broken down by sport, bet type, timing, and more.

Is Wysely free?

Yes — the core features are completely free. You can track an unlimited number of bets, view your dashboard, and see your overall performance at no cost. Wysely Pro ($4/month or $40/year) unlocks advanced analytics including the Sport × Bet Type matrix, ROI vs Volume scatter plot, Expected vs Actual win rate analysis, and more.

Does Wysely let me place bets?

No. Wysely is a read-only tracker. It records and analyzes your existing betting history — it cannot place bets, access your sportsbook funds, or make any transactions on your behalf.

Where is Wysely available?

Wysely is available as a web app at app.wyse-ly.com — accessible from any browser on any device. An iOS app is coming soon.

What type of sports bettor is Wysely for?

Wysely was created with the mildly-serious but non-professional sports bettor in mind. Someone who bets regularly with the goal to make a modest return, but needs help optimizing their personal betting strategy.

Syncing & Sportsbooks

Does Wysely work with FanDuel?

Yes. Wysely syncs your FanDuel bet history automatically using the Wysely Chrome Extension. Once installed, open FanDuel in Chrome, click the extension, and your settled bets sync into Wysely instantly.

Does Wysely work with DraftKings?

Yes. The Wysely Chrome Extension also supports DraftKings. The sync process is the same — open DraftKings in Chrome, click the extension, and your bets appear in Wysely within seconds.

Will Wysely support other sportsbooks in the future?

Most likely, yes. We plan to add additional sportsbooks to our syncing capabilities at some point in the future. That said, manual entry and CSV import work for any sportsbook — if you can export your bet history, you can get it into Wysely.

What if I don't use the Chrome extension?

Wysely is fully functional without the extension. You can add bets manually from the Add Bet tab, or import a bulk history using a CSV file. While the manual entry flow supports all major sports and bet types, we highly recommend setting up the Chrome extension. It takes a few minutes the first time and makes your life much easier in the long run.

Does syncing access my payment information?

No. The Chrome extension only reads your settled bet history — descriptions, odds, stakes, and outcomes. It cannot access your balance, payment methods, or personal account credentials.

Analytics & Features

What metrics does Wysely track?

Wysely tracks ROI, win rate, total profit/loss, average stake, and bet counts — broken down by sport, bet type, season, timing (pre-season vs mid-season), and game context (regular season vs playoffs). Pro users also get cross-reference matrices, a scatter plot comparing ROI vs betting volume, and an expected vs actual win rate analysis by odds range.

Will Wysely track additional metrics in the future?

Yes, in the future we will add additional metrics, most likely within the Pro tier.

How is ROI calculated in Wysely?

ROI is calculated as total profit/loss divided by total amount wagered across all bets, expressed as a percentage. This matches the standard definition used by professional bettors and mirrors the calculation that will be used in the iOS app.

What is the Sport × Bet Type matrix?

This is a Pro feature that shows your ROI for every combination of sport and bet type in a grid. Each cell shows your ROI and number of bets for that pairing — for example, how you perform on NFL Spread bets vs NFL Moneyline bets. Cells are shaded green if your ROI exceeds your overall average and red if it falls below, making it easy to spot where you have an edge.

Privacy & Security

Is my betting data private?

Yes. Your data is stored securely in your personal account and is never shared with third parties or used for any purpose other than powering your own analytics. Each user's data is completely isolated — no one else can see your bets or performance.

Does Wysely sell my data?

No. Wysely does not sell, share, or monetize your personal data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What happens to my data if I delete my account?

Deleting your account permanently removes all your data from Wysely's servers. This action cannot be undone. You can delete your account from the Account tab in the app.

Account & Billing

How do I upgrade to Wysely Pro?

You can upgrade directly from the Account tab in the web app. Pro is available for $4/month or $40/year (save 17%). Your Pro subscription works across both the web app and the iOS app with the same account.

Can I use the same account on web and iOS?

Yes. Your Wysely account, bets, and Pro subscription are shared across the web app and the iOS app. Any bets you add on one platform appear instantly on the other.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from the Account tab → Manage Subscription. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period.
