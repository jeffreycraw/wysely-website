Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 23, 2026

1. Introduction

CoDi & Co Ventures LLC ("we", "our", "us") operates Wysely ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication).

Betting Data: When you use our browser extension to sync with supported sportsbooks, we import your betting history including bet types, stakes, odds, outcomes, and event details. This data is stored in your private account and is not shared with third parties.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the App, such as feature usage patterns and crash reports.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Sync and display your betting history
  • Generate personalized analytics and performance insights
  • Process subscription payments (via Apple)
  • Communicate important updates about the App
  • Improve and optimize the App experience

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and data storage
  • Apple StoreKit: Subscription payment processing

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

6. Browser Extension

The Wysely Chrome extension reads your betting history from supported sportsbook websites (currently FanDuel) when you are logged in. The extension only accesses betting data and authentication tokens necessary for syncing. It does not collect browsing history, passwords, or any data from non-sportsbook websites.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account and betting data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account through the App, all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the App
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your betting data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 21. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 21. If we discover that we have collected information from a minor, we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or via email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at hello@codicoventures.com.
